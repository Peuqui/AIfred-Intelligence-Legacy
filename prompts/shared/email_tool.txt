Manage the user's emails. Actions: check (list inbox), read (full email by msg_id), search (find by subject/sender), delete (remove by msg_id), send (draft an email). You MUST use this tool for ALL email operations — checking, reading, deleting, searching, sending. Never pretend you performed an email action without calling this tool.
